How To Play

Demons are known to taunt mortals who trespass their realms. You can dismiss these taunts by clicking/tapping on the taunt or hitting 'Enter' on your keyboard.

As punishment for trespassing, these Demons will ask a question of you. Use the your finger/mouse, or the 'WASD'/Arrow Keys to select your answer. Tap/Click twice or use 'Enter' to make your choice, convincing the Demon you belong with a correct answer.

Be wary as mistakes are costly and Demons enjoy draining the life force from mortals, such as yourself.

Press 'Escape'/'Tab' or click the button that will appear in the top-right when you begin at any time to bring up this menu and start over.
